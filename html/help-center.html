How Weighting Works

Your custom weights recalculate overall scores for all sectors and assets:

Overall Score = (Safety × w1) + (Growth × w2) + (Volatility × w3) + (ESG × w4)
Safety weights: capital preservation, stable cash flow
Growth weights: opportunity, innovation, expansion
Volatility weights: uncertainty, risk, price swings
ESG weights: environmental, social, governance factors
