OUR SERVICES

Decode Market Emotions in Real Time

Traditional sentiment tools tell you what people are saying but not what it means. They offer a single positive or negative score, ignore emotional nuance, and are built for institutions, not individual investors. ORION analyze millions of data points, such as news, social media, filings, to deliver multi-dimensional sentiment intelligence that actually helps you understand market perception.

WORK PROCESS

Four Sentiment Dimensions

01

Safety

Capital preservation, stability eg:"Is this asset considered a safe haven?"

02

Growth

Opportunity, upside potential eg:"Is the market optimistic about future earnings?"

03

volatility

Uncertainty, risk

eg:"Are people worried about price swings?"

04

ESG

Responsibility, sustainability eg:"How is the company viewed on environmental/social issues?"
